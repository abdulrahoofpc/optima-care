Home and Living

We help you live as independently as possible with diƯerent types of home and living support. The kind of support you receive will be included in your plan.

Medium-Term Accommodation (MTA) – HProvides a temporary home for up to 90 days if your permanent housing isn’t ready due to disability-related needs. You must have a long-term home planned to qualify.

Individualised Living Option (ILO) – Lets you choose where and how you live, with tailored support to meet your needs. Support can include personal care, skill development, and help with daily tasks like cooking and shopping. Friends, family, and other networks can also complement paid support.

Residential Aged Care Support (YPIRAC) – Provides extra funding for younger people in aged care facilities who need additional support with daily care and accommodation due to their disability.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access your services?

You can contact us directly via our website, phone, or email to discuss your needs, and our team will assist you in creating a personalized care plan.

Do you offer tailored care plans?

Yes, we provide personalized care solutions based on individual needs, ensuring the highest level of support and care.

What makes OptimaCare Solutions different from other care providers?

Our mission is to deliver high-quality, compassionate, and innovative care that empowers individuals and communities. We focus on personalized solutions, accessibility, and community-driven support.

How can I get more information or book a consultation?

You can reach out to us via our website or contact our support team for more details on our services and how we can assist you.

What kind of transport services do you offer?

We provide reliable transport assistance for medical appointments, social events, and daily activities, ensuring safe and comfortable travel for our clients.
