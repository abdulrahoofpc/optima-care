Frequently Asked Questions

What services does OptimaCare Solutions provide?

OptimaCare Solutions offers Assist Travel Transport, Community Nursing, Daily Personal Activities, Household Tasks, and Behaviour Support, ensuring individuals receive high-quality, compassionate care.

How can I access transportation assistance?

Our Assist Travel Transport service provides safe and reliable travel for medical appointments, therapy sessions, social events, and errands. Contact us to arrange transportation based on your needs.

What is the difference between Daily Personal Activities and High Intensity support?

Daily Personal Activities covers routine tasks like bathing and mobility support, while High Intensity support is for individuals with complex medical needs, requiring specialized care and medical assistance.

How does Behaviour Support help?

We provide personalized strategies to manage challenging behaviors, emotional regulation, and communication difficulties, working with individuals and caregivers to enhance well-being and social interactions.

Can you help with community participation?

Yes! Our Participate Community and Group Centre-Based Activities encourage individuals to engage in social events, recreational activities, volunteer work, and employment opportunities, fostering independence and confidence.
